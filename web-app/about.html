About AtScale

AtScale was founded by members of the data team at Yahoo in 2013. They began working on the AtScale semantic layer after working on big data from the trenches – dealing with the basic challenges of data scale, query performance, metrics sprawl, complicated data pipelines and shadow BI. AtScale takes a highly unique approach to accelerating time to insights, enabling data literacy, and driving value from AI/ML and data assets

AtScale is focused on helping customers create value from their data and AI/ML investments

AtScale enables smarter decision-making by accelerating the flow of data-driven insights. The company’s semantic layer platform simplifies, accelerates, and extends business intelligence and data science capabilities for enterprise customers across all industries. With AtScale, customers are empowered to democratize data, implement self-service BI and build a more agile analytics infrastructure for better, more impactful decision making.
